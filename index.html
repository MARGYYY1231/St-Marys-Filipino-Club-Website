Boracay

The tiny island of Boracay is one of those places where you can stay happliy marooned for a very long time. Boracay is known as one of the best beach destinations in the world! Boracay has dazziling beaches lined with palm trees, splendid sunshine, warm crystaline waters and it has a lot of of activities on offer.

Manila

The Philippines capital of Manila,is a bustling city with non-stop activity. In Manila you should try and hop on board one of the colorful jeepneys which is the main form of public transportation.If you go there it is best if you go to their top attractions as I am sure that you will enjoy!

About the Phillipines



The Philippines is a tropical country in Pacific Ocean, it has many beautiful landsacpes like which is seen in some of the slideshow images above. Philippies have beautiful landsacpes but it also has active volcanoes and extreme weather patterns. The Philippines is located in the western Pacific ocean and it has 7,107 islands.
